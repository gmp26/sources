Show that the equation of any circle passing through the points of intersection of the ellipse
$$(x + 2)^2 + 2y^2 = 18$$
and the ellipse
$$9(x - 1)^2 + 16y^2 = 25$$
can be written in the form
$$x^2 - 2ax + y^2 = 5 - 4a.$$
